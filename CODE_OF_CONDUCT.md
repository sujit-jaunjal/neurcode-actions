# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and give actionable feedback.
- Focus on facts, reproducibility, and user impact.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discrimination.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Disruptive, hostile, or abusive communication.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove or edit contributions and moderate participation when needed to protect the community.

For serious concerns, contact `conduct@neurcode.com`.
